Mi Sangre

Mi Sangre #

Date: 2022-10-18
Location: Design Media Arts, University of California, Los Angeles

Overview #

Interactive artwork encouraging new ways of composing music. Working with Unity to create custom sound interfaces, on start, orbs with sound loops are spread in this virtual world. The audience (user) uses a controller to navigate the space, explore the sounds. One is encouraged to explore this space and find sounds or combinations of sounds that are appealing.
